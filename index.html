Center for Advanced Imaging Innovation and Research (CAI2R)

CAI2R research is primarily focused on magnetic resonance imaging (MRI).

CAI2R also explore positron emission tomography (PET), computed tomography (CT), and ultrasound (US), alone or in combination with MRI.
